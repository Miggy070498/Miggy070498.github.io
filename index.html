My Projects

Amazon Review Classifier

Amazon receives thousands of reviews of products almost every single day. In this project, I implemented multiple models to classify whether a reviews is positive or negative.

Cat and Dog Classifier

Uhh.. meow? In this project, I trained a convolutional neural network to classify whether an image is a cat or a dog.

Churn Prediction

Churn dictates how many customers will stop using a certain product, service, or subscription from a business. Using a logistic regreddion model, I determined if certain customers will churn or not.

Employee Attrition

Attrition is the loss of an employee through natural means such as retirement, resignation, health etc. However, the position vacated will not be filled by the employer. Here, a random forest classifier was trained.

Supermarket Data Analysis

Here, I perform a series of analysis on a supermarker dataset, gather insights, and illustrate these through graphs.

Grade Predictor

If you're like me when I was a student, you'd want to find out what your grades were in advanced. In this, a linear regression model was trained to predict the grades of a population of students.

Movie Recommendation Engine

Seen a movie and want to find films similar to it? This model uses K Nearest Neighbors to determine how "similar" certain movies are and creates a top 10 movies recommendation list.

Twitter Sentiment Classifier

If you want to find out if your partner is being passive aggressive, then let a machine find out for you. This model uses Naive Bayes to determine if certain tweets are in a positive or negative light.
